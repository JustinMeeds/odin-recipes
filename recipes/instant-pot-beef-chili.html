Instant Pot Beef Chili Recipe

Description

This Instant Pot beef chili is a quick and hearty one-pot meal packed with bold flavors. Ground beef is combined with beans, tomatoes, and spices to create a thick and satisfying chili in a fraction of the time it takes to cook traditionally.

It’s perfect for meal prep or freezing, and the flavors get even better as it sits. Whether you serve it with sour cream, shredded cheese, or tortilla chips, this chili is bound to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef: 500g (1.1 lbs)
  • Olive oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Onion: 1 medium, chopped
  • Bell pepper: 1, diced
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Canned diced tomatoes: 1 can (400g / 15 oz)
  • Tomato paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Kidney beans: 1 can (400g / 15 oz), drained and rinsed
  • Black beans: 1 can (400g / 15 oz), drained and rinsed
  • Frozen corn: 1 cup
  • Beef broth: 1 cup
  • Red wine: 1/2 cup (optional)
  • Chili powder: 1 tablespoon
  • Ground cumin: 1 teaspoon
  • Paprika: 1 teaspoon
  • Oregano: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and black pepper: To taste

Steps

  1. Set the Instant Pot to Sauté mode. Add olive oil and ground beef, and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Sauté for 3-5 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, and oregano, cooking for 1 minute to release the flavors.
  4. Deglaze the pot with red wine (if using), scraping up any browned bits.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, kidney beans, and beef broth. Stir well and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Close the lid and set the Instant Pot to Pressure Cook on High for 20 minutes.
  7. Once the cooking is complete, allow a natural release for 10 minutes, then carefully do a quick release.
  8. Open the lid, stir the chili, and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot with your favorite toppings like sour cream, shredded cheese, or tortilla chips.
